The State of Diversity in Film

A closer look at 10 top films from 2016

The Story

The 2016 films honored at the 2017 Oscars made it the most diverse Oscars yet. But should Hollywood be patting themselves on the back just yet? We wanted to know: how diverse are these top films really, both in terms of casting and production?
